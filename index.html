What We Do

Our Services

From acute injury to peak performance — tailored treatment built around your goals and your body.

01

Hands-On Physiotherapy

Manual therapy including joint mobilisations, soft tissue massage and targeted stretching — tailored to your body's specific needs to relieve pain and improve movement.

Learn more →
02

Dry Needling Therapy

Fine needles target muscle trigger points to release tension, ease pain and improve movement. Quick, effective and can be booked as a standalone treatment or part of your plan.

Learn more →
03

Running Assessment

Treadmill analysis of running technique combined with strength, power and mobility testing to build a personalised plan that helps you run stronger, faster, and injury-free.

Learn more →
04

Post-Surgery Rehab

Coordinated recovery in close communication with your surgeon. Hands-on treatment, targeted exercises and regular progress checks to get you back to full strength safely.

Learn more →
05

NormaTec Recovery

Compression boot sessions to boost circulation, reduce soreness and speed up recovery after hard training or a long day on your feet. No appointment needed — just show up.

Learn more →
06

Tailored Gym Programs

Personalised programs using our fully equipped in-clinic gym. Assessment of strength, movement and technique ensures every exercise is right for your body and your goals.

Meet Your Physio

Adrian Primerano

Principal Physiotherapist & Director

Adrian is a qualified physiotherapist with a solid background in sports and competition. He understands what it takes to push your limits — and how frustrating it is when your body doesn't keep up.

With over 13 years working with athletes, Adrian brings a whole-body, athlete-first approach to treatment. He doesn't just treat the injury — he looks at everything from movement and recovery to nutrition and training load.
